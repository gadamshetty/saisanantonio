Sai Baba Temple of San Antonio

Sri Shirdi Sai Baba, a saint of the present century of indian origin practiced multi religious philosophies of Hinduism, Christianity and Muslim religion encouraging oneness of God. baba lived to unite people from different ethnicities, religions and to inspire everyone realize oneness with God. Baba promised to the suffering humanity, material success happiness and prosperity through his eleven solemn assurances.

Shirdi Sai Devotional Group of San Antonio was started by a group of ardent devotees of Sri Shirdi Sai baba in the month of December, 2013. Our only mission is to religiously follow teachings and principles of Sai Baba and serve the community.
